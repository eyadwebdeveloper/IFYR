Welcome, to IFYR!


Since YSJ's very first moments, our mission was to be a platform promoting research worldwide. Advancing this mission, we enthusiastically announce the first version of IFYR. IFYR is aimed to provide a supportive atmosphere for youth researchers globally, highlighting their research work and proposing solutions to pressing world problems.

Following peer review, the first round results will be uploaded on this application portal. In case your team is qualified to the second round, you will be asked to submit a presentation highlighting the most important aspects of your research for the second round. Please read the competition guidelines carefully before applying.

If you are still working on your research article, you can seek help from our research administrators on the competition discord channel on YSJ discord server.
Best regards to all applicants.
